How to Fade Hair Barber School Inc.


BARBER SCHOOL / escuela de peluquero

Located in the Town of Cicero, How To Fade Hair Barber School is Chicagolands #1 choice.
Licensed by the State of Illinois.
Our mission is to maintain a commitment to excellence in our programs.
To provide quality instruction to all students.
To provide a safe, supportive and energetic environment for our students that facilitates the knowledge, skills and confidence necessary to attain success in their field.
To assist our graduates in securing employment in their field which will allow them to be responsible, contributing factors to society. We provide all kinds of modern and innovative services.

All work is done by students under supervision of an instructor.
